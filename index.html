El Pollo Loco

Story

Somewhere in a Mexican desert, the brave Pepe Peligroso is confronted with a horde of strangely upset chickens. According to rumors, they have tasted a mysterious salsa sauce made from secret special ingredients, which caused them to start losing their minds.

Help Pepe make his way through the desert. Along you may find salsa bottles lying around that you can use to defend yourself. You should also collect as many coins as possible, since they give you energy and sparkle lovely in the sunlight.

But beware: stories have been told about one insane, aggressive psycho chicken roaming nearby, which is claimed to be bigger than anything ever encountered: "El Pollo Loco"...
